## ðŸŽ¨ Phase 2: The Imagination (`!SHAPE`)
**The Novel Core:**
"We create a **Paw-Quiet Path** system that helps a child pick a calmer route and quietly alert a trusted adult without calling attention."

**The Design:**
* **Input:** A simple button on a phone or watch: `Need a safe path`.
* **Logic:** The app suggests a nearby calmer route using recent community reports and time-of-day patterns.
* **Interoperability:** It sends a gentle check-in to a trusted contact or school buddy so someone knows the child is changing route.
