# Bamboo-Based Scaffolding for Affordable Construction in Rural India

# 1. Spark Narrative

Bamboo scaffolding offers a safe, sustainable, and affordable alternative to steel for construction projects in rural India. Steel scaffolding is often prohibitively expensive, leading painters and workers to use unsafe techniques such as ladders, ropes, or improvised platforms. By leveraging locally available bamboo and proper treatment methods, communities can improve safety, reduce costs, and support sustainable building practices.
